Qualified Clinical Nutritionist & Management Professional

With over 36 years of dedicated experience in clinical nutrition and dietetics, Dr. Padamaja Joshi stands as a pioneer in critical care nutrition, particularly in burn and trauma patient care.

Dr. Joshi has served as Chief Dietician in prestigious multispecialty hospitals including Choithram Hospital & Research Center (Indore), Jupiter Lifeline Hospitals (Thane), and H J Doshi Hospital (Mumbai).

Her groundbreaking research on nutritional support in burn patients has earned her multiple national awards including the K.G. Naidu Medical Trust Award and A.N. Radha Practicing Dietician Award.
